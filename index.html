IP PBXs & Gateways

In IP PBX systems, a gateway is used to connect the system to external networks like PSTN or cellular networks. The gateway converts analog or digital signals to IP signals, enabling calls to be transmitted over the internet or LAN. IP technology and gateways are crucial components of IP PBX systems and other communication systems like VoIP.
