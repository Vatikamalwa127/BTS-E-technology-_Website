IP PBXs and Gateway with Accessories

In IVR (Interactive Voice Response) and FreePBX systems, gateways and IP phones play an important role in enabling communication between different networks and facilitating the flow of information. A gateway is a device that connects the IVR or FreePBX system to external networks, such as the Public Switched Telephone Network (PSTN) or cellular networks. The gateway enables the IVR or FreePBX system to route calls to and from these external networks. IP phones are used in IVR and FreePBX systems to provide a user-friendly interface for making and receiving calls. They use internet protocol (IP) technology to transmit voice and data over the internet or a local area network (LAN). In an IVR system, callers interact with the system by pressing keys on their phone or by using voice commands.
